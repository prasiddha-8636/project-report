\chapter{EXPECTED RESULTS}
The final system will take a mountain photograph as input and return a predicted location and heading. For each query, the output includes the top-ranked matching coordinates on a map, the extracted skyline overlaid against the best-matching database horizon, and a confidence score derived from the DTW alignment cost. Testing on Google Street View imagery from the Khumbu trails \cite{google2015streetview} will measure how often the correct grid cell appears in the top-$k$ results.
